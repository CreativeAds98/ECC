Founded in 2018 in Tirupur, India, East Coast Corp was born from a simple promise: to create the gentlest touch for the world's most delicate skin. As a 100% Export Oriented Unit, we don't just manufacture garments; we engineer comfort.

From sourcing the purest organic cotton to our ISO-certified finishing processes, every romper, swaddle, and mitten is a testament to our obsession with quality. We are the silent partner behind premium global brands, dedicated to sustainability and the art of soft living.
